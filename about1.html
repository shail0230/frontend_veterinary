About Little Heart: Caring for Animals Since 2012

Our Mission

At Little Heart, our mission is simple yet profound: to be a beacon of hope and compassion for animals in need. Founded in 2012, we've dedicated ourselves to providing exceptional veterinary care and offering refuge to animals awaiting their forever homes.

Our Journey

*In the Beginning*: Little Heart had humble beginnings, born out of a deep love for animals and a desire to make a meaningful impact. It started as a dream in a small clinic, where our founder, Dr. Ankit C. Yadav, recognized the need for both quality veterinary services and a sanctuary for animals without a home. *Growing with Purpose*: Over the years, our organization has grown, embracing the belief that every animal deserves a life filled with love, care, and dignity. We've expanded our services, facilities, and team to better serve the community and the animals we hold dear.

Our Core Values

At Little Heart, we are guided by our unwavering commitment to: -

Compassion

: We believe in treating every animal with the utmost compassion and respect, recognizing their unique needs and individuality. -

Excellence

: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from providing top-notch veterinary care to maintaining safe and loving environments for our shelter residents. -

Community

: We view our community as an integral part of our mission, working together to promote responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.

Our Services

We wear two hats: as a leading veterinary clinic and an animal shelter. -

Veterinary Care

: Our veterinary clinic offers a wide range of services, from routine check-ups to advanced medical treatments. We're dedicated to ensuring your pets live happy, healthy lives. -

Animal Shelter

: Our shelter is a haven for animals in need. We provide them with shelter, nourishment, and the opportunity to find loving homes. Our team works tirelessly to match animals with the right families.

Our Vision for the Future

Our journey has just begun, and we have a vision of expanding our impact. We aim to: - Continue offering top-tier veterinary care to our community. - Grow our shelter, providing more animals with safe, loving environments. - Educate and engage our community in responsible pet ownership and animal welfare.
